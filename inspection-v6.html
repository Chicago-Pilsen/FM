🛞 Tire Condition Inspection
Record tread depth, DOT date, and condition for each tire. Tread depth in 32nds of an inch — legal minimum is 2/32", replace at 4/32".
DOT Date Guide: Found on the tire sidewall — last 4 digits of the DOT code. First 2 = week, last 2 = year. Example: DOT …2319 = week 23 of 2019. Tires over 6 years old should be inspected; over 10 years replace regardless of tread.
